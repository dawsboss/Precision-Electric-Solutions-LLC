What We Do

Services

From small repairs to full installs, we handle it all with clean work and clear communication.

Residential

New construction wiring, rewires, outlets, switches, ceiling fans, lighting, and everything your home needs to stay safe and up to code.

Commercial

Offices, retail spaces, and light industrial. We keep your business powered and compliant — minimal downtime, maximum reliability.

Panel Upgrades

Old 100A panel slowing you down? We upgrade to 200A and beyond, safely and to code — future-proofing your home or business.
